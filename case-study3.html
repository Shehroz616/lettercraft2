CASE STUDY ON
MEDICAL TRANSLATION
Letter Crafts successfully translated medical documents for a top insurance provider in Singapore, covering Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Thai languages. Despite content variations, we formed a skilled team, implemented a three-step translation process, created a validated technical glossary, and used quality control tools. The result: accurate and timely submission of individual documents for insurance claims processing.
